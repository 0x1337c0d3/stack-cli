Initial setup — iPhone or iPad
1

Open Journal app (comes with iOS 17.2+)

If missing: App Store → search "Journal" → install the Apple one

2

Tap Get Started → allow notifications when prompted

You want the morning and evening reminders — set them in the next step

3

Go to Settings app → Journal

Set two reminders: Morning at 8:00 am · Evening at 8:30 pm

4

Back in Journal → tap + → choose New Entry

Ignore the suggested prompts for now — you'll use the ones below instead

💡 Journal entries are end-to-end encrypted on device and locked with Face ID. Nothing syncs to iCloud unless you enable it — it's genuinely private.
Morning entry — 3 minutes
☀️ Morning intention ~3 min · 8:00 am

What is the one thing that would make today feel successful?

Single answer only — forces prioritisation

What's weighing on me that I should put in Reminders right now?

Capture to Inbox immediately — clear the mental RAM

Who do I need to connect with or follow up on today?

Optional — skip if nothing comes to mind

Evening entry — 5 minutes
🌙 Evening review ~5 min · 8:30 pm

Three things that went well today — however small

Non-negotiable: always three, even on bad days

What didn't happen today that needs to move forward?

Open Reminders and move it to This Week or Someday now

One thing I want to remember or think about tomorrow

Optional — a thought, feeling, or idea worth keeping

✅ The evening review is the glue. When you skip it, tasks pile up in your head overnight. Even 2 minutes is enough — just answer the first two prompts.
Weekly review — Sunday evening, 15 minutes

Brain dump

What's still floating in my head that isn't captured anywhere?

Reminders

Clear done items · Move stale tasks to Someday · Set up Today for Monday

Look back

What was the highlight of this week? What drained me most?

Look ahead

What are the 3 most important things for next week?

💡 Create a new Notes scratch pad note on Sunday too — title it with next week's number (e.g. "W15 2026") so it's ready Monday morning.
How the three apps work together
AM Journal → set intention → dump anything unplanned into Reminders Inbox
Day Reminders for tasks · Notes for thinking and meeting notes
PM Journal → review → update Reminders lists for tomorrow
